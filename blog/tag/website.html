Website Launched

 Tue 16 April 2013 by Chris

We've Launched!

Wow, our first big milestone - launching of the KidsCanCode.org website!

It's literally been just over three weeks since we decided to make this happen, and it's been a crazy time. We have dozens of balls in the air right now, but here are some highlights of what's going on:

  • I'm teaching and trying out some of our class content with a group of 5th graders at Monlux Elementary starting April 24th.
  • We've booked a location for summer classes - starting in June: the Sherman Oaks Galleria Community Room. It's a really nice space - and perfect location!
  • Registration will be up for June sessions within the week.
  • Presentation for Girl Scouts STEM program in 2 weeks.

Thanks to everyone who's provided feedback (or will soon). Hopefully I'll have time to update this blog in between all of the million things that are involved with getting a business up and running.

PS - Please also check us out on FB and Twitter (links on the right) and like/follow.
